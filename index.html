About

Experience that
spans the room

One of the things I've always enjoyed most about enterprise sales is sitting at the intersection of technical depth and business impact. I genuinely like understanding how engineers and architects think about problems — and then helping translate those conversations into the language of ROI, risk, and strategic value for the executives making buying decisions.

Over the years, I've built relationships across both sides of the table — earning trust with technical stakeholders who appreciate that I take the time to understand their world, and delivering outcomes for business leaders who need clear answers on return.

Beyond SaaS, I opened and grew a physical therapy franchise — handling everything from buildout and staffing to billing, compliance, and patient experience. Taking it from zero to $500K in year one while integrating software across every function gave me an operator's perspective that informs how I approach every engagement.

I'm also increasingly building with AI — shipping real tools and applications that solve real problems. It's an area I find genuinely exciting, and one I think is going to reshape how sales and revenue teams operate over the next few years.
